# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. The dashboard provides key insights into employee demographics, job roles, salary distribution, and attrition patterns, enabling HR teams to make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and organizational performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* 📈 Analyze employee attrition trends.
* 🏢 Identify departments and job roles with higher attrition.
* 👥 Understand workforce demographics.
* 💰 Evaluate salary and income distribution.
* 📊 Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* 📊 **Power BI**
* 📑 **Excel / CSV**
* 🔄 **Power Query**
* 📈 **Data Visualization**

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains employee-related information, including:

* 👤 Employee Demographics
* 🏢 Department
* 💼 Job Role
* 🎓 Education Field
* 💵 Monthly Income
* 📅 Years at Company
* 😊 Job Satisfaction
* 🚪 Attrition Status

## 🚀 Key Insights

✅ Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians show relatively higher attrition.

✅ Employees with lower monthly income tend to have higher attrition rates.

✅ Most attrition occurs among employees with fewer years at the company.

✅ Workforce demographics and education fields influence retention patterns.
